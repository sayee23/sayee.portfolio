About

Sayee is a visual communicator, UI/UX designer, and creative educator. Her design blossoms from back-end research, understanding the psychology of the user, with minute detailing. She is a nature lover, who has travelled valleys, hiked mountains, and opines that nature bestows opportunities for her to observe, grasp, develop, process the learnings, and produce a creative output. As she moves ahead in her design journey, with full enthusiasm and energy, she has kept no boundaries to her creative thinking and is open and keen on adopting new skills, exploring new cultures, and broadening her horizons, to the optimum.
